MISHAEL MORGAN

Tribute Page

Background

If you do not watch Young & Restless, then what are you doing with your life. Joking... Hey everyone, this is a tribute page dedicated to my favorite actress by the name of Mishael Morgan. Misheal Morgan was born and raised in San Fernando,Trinidad & Tobago and relocated to Toronto,Canada with her family and studied at Univeristy of Ottawa. Later into her acting career, she received her role as Hilary Curtis on the Daytime Emmy show Young & Restless. Although Hilary Curtis isn't everyone's favorite resident of Genoa City in the show, her taste in fashion says otherwise. Shout out to Misheal Morgan representing Caribbean women in the mainstream.
